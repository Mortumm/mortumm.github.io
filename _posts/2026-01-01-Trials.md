The past months have really been a test of resilience and mental fortitude. 

Work has had its moments, both my day job and my entrepreneurship have been more than challenging and rewarding. New acquisitions in systems have been a process to onboard, fittingly preparing us for bigger changes coming in the first quarter of 2026. Something for all of us to adjust to, not only in the technical sense of things. 

For my entreprenurial endeavours, PaaS and SaaS solutions have been the most time consuming projects. I am more than happy to help my customers to find a leaner application, for their umm, applications. 

Studies have been a bit slower during the year, I'm looking to pick up speed for the second part of the year and really cranking it up for the 4th year. 

The most challenging parts though, have risen from my personal life: The uncontrollable, unforeseen changes and challenges. A great way to benchmark your resilience!   
Taking a moment to slow down and see what is the personal plan looking like for the next two to three years.

Eyes up, keep reaching for the stars (what ever it personally is).
